About MHT CET

MHT CET (Maharashtra Common Entrance Test) is a state-level entrance exam for admission to engineering and pharmacy courses in Maharashtra. The exam is conducted by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra.

Exam Pattern: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology (100 questions each, 100 marks each)

Duration: 90 minutes per section

About JEE

JEE (Joint Entrance Examination) is a national level engineering entrance exam conducted for admission to various engineering colleges in India, including NITs, IIITs, and other Centrally Funded Technical Institutions (CFTIs).

Exam Pattern: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (25 questions each, 100 marks each)

Duration: 3 hours
